WELCOME TO MOZILLA

Mozilla Phoenix Club is a group of students at Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology with a passion for technology who meet regularly to advance Mozilla’s mission by building and innovating on open source projects that keep the web open. Also learn development skills and then showcasing their work within their communities.

About Mozilla

Open Source & Non-Profit Organization
Best known for Firefox Browser and Firefox OS.
A community of 40,000+ volunteers working worldwide on a mission of “creating free open and innovative web”.
Formed on 28th February 1998 by Netscape Communications as Mozilla Organization

Web Development

Web development builds and maintains websites. It combines HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for user interfaces, with server-side technologies managing data and logic.

App Development

App development is creating software applications for mobile or desktop platforms. It involves coding, designing, and testing to produce functional and user-friendly applications.

Programing

Programming is the creation of executable code using specific languages. It involves problem-solving, logic, and designing algorithms to build software applications.
